Welcome to Ebenezer SHS

Ebenezer Senior High School has been a beacon of educational excellence since its establishment in 1941 by Robert Teiko Aryee. Located in the vibrant community of Dansoman, Accra, our institution has consistently produced graduates who excel in academics, sports, and co-curricular activities.

Our commitment to holistic education, combined with state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated educators, ensures that every student receives the best possible preparation for their future endeavors.

About Ebenezer SHS

Our History

Ebenezer Senior High School was founded in 1941 by Robert Teiko Aryee, a visionary educator committed to providing quality secondary education to students in Dansoman and surrounding communities. Over eight decades, the school has grown from humble beginnings to become one of the most respected educational institutions in Ghana.

Our Mission

To provide quality, inclusive, and innovative education that develops well-rounded individuals equipped with knowledge, skills, and values necessary to contribute meaningfully to society and compete globally.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution of excellence in secondary education, recognized for academic achievement, character development, and the production of responsible citizens and leaders.

Academic Programmes

Ebenezer SHS offers six comprehensive academic programmes designed to cater to diverse student interests and career aspirations:

General Science

Focuses on Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Ideal for students pursuing careers in medicine, engineering, and natural sciences.

Visual Arts

Emphasizes creative expression through painting, sculpture, and design. Perfect for aspiring artists and designers.

Home Economics

Covers nutrition, hospitality management, and consumer studies. Great for careers in hospitality and home management.

Business

Prepares students for commerce and entrepreneurship with courses in accounting, economics, and business management.

Agriculture Science

Combines theoretical knowledge with practical farming techniques. Suitable for agricultural careers and agribusiness.

General Arts

Focuses on humanities including literature, history, and social studies. Ideal for careers in law, journalism, and education.

Admissions

Admission Process

Our admission process is transparent and merit-based. Follow these steps:

  1. Application: Submit your application form with required documents
  2. Entrance Examination: Take our entrance examination in English, Mathematics, and Science
  3. Interview: Participate in a brief interview with our admissions team
  4. Selection: Successful candidates are notified of admission
  5. Registration: Complete registration and pay fees to secure your place

Requirements

  • Birth certificate or national ID
  • Primary/Junior High School leaving certificate
  • Recent passport-sized photograph (4x6)
  • Parent/Guardian contact information
  • Medical report (optional but recommended)
